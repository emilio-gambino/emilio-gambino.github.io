Travel adventures in China

After leaving High-School, I decided to leave my hometown and move to Siping city in Northern China to train Shaolin KungFu. I have an article on how life was at the KungFu school. After training for a few months, my good friend Jony and I went on a big trip around China. We wanted to visit as much as possible of China in two weeks time so we visited during the day and slept on trains (sometimes over 30 hours long) during the nights.

Kunming (Zhangjiajie)

1. Tianmen Shan: Cable car is very close to the train station, go one day (the day you arrive or the day you leave). Glass walkway is overrated, other walkways are better. 5 hours. 2. Zhangjiajie National Forest Park: 4-day ticket, stay in Wulingyuan while visiting this park [you can take a bus from the Zhangjiajie train station for about 14 kuai], my favorite part was hiking up and down Tianzi Shan [there were basically no people]. 2 days. 3. Grand Canyon Park: if you have extra time and money or get sick of climbing stairs, this park is also really nice with cool caves--if you go, don't just do the glass bridge, it's the least cool part. 4 hours. add mountains.mp4
